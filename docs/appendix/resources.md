# Further Reading & Resources

## Academic Journals

### Top-Tier Robotics Journals
- **Science Robotics**: Premier journal for cutting-edge robotics research
- **IEEE Transactions on Robotics (T-RO)**: Leading venue for theoretical and experimental robotics research
- **International Journal of Robotics Research (IJRR)**: High-impact journal covering all aspects of robotics
- **Autonomous Robots**: Focuses on autonomous systems and their applications
- **Journal of Field Robotics**: Emphasizes robotics systems operating in real-world environments
- **Robotics and Autonomous Systems**: Covers both theoretical and practical aspects of robotics

### AI and Machine Learning Journals
- **Journal of Machine Learning Research (JMLR)**: Open-access journal for machine learning research
- **Neural Computation**: Covers neural networks, learning, and adaptive systems
- **IEEE Transactions on Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence (TPAMI)**: Leading computer vision and pattern recognition journal
- **Nature Machine Intelligence**: High-impact venue for AI research with societal impact

## Conference Proceedings

### Major Robotics Conferences
- **ICRA (IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation)**: Premier conference for robotics research
- **IROS (IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems)**: International conference focusing on intelligent robotics
- **RSS (Robotics: Science and Systems)**: Academic conference emphasizing scientific rigor in robotics
- **ISER (International Symposium on Experimental Robotics)**: Focuses on experimental validation of robotics research
- **ISRR (International Symposium on Robotics Research)**: Theoretical and experimental robotics research symposium

### AI and Machine Learning Conferences
- **NeurIPS (Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems)**: Leading conference in machine learning and computational neuroscience
- **ICML (International Conference on Machine Learning)**: Premier venue for machine learning research
- **CVPR (Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition)**: Top conference for computer vision research
- **ICLR (International Conference on Learning Representations)**: Focuses on deep learning and representation learning

## Books and Textbooks

### Core Robotics Textbooks
- **"Robotics: Modelling, Planning and Control"** by Siciliano et al.: Comprehensive textbook covering fundamental robotics concepts
- **"Probabilistic Robotics"** by Thrun, Burgard, and Fox: Definitive text on probabilistic approaches in robotics
- **"Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control"** by Lynch and Park: Modern approach to robotics with focus on mathematical foundations
- **"Introduction to Autonomous Mobile Robots"** by Siegwart et al.: Excellent introduction to mobile robotics
- **"Springer Handbook of Robotics"** edited by Siciliano and Khatib: Comprehensive reference for robotics research

### AI and Machine Learning Textbooks
- **"Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning"** by Christopher Bishop: Comprehensive introduction to machine learning
- **"Deep Learning"** by Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville: Definitive text on deep learning
- **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"** by Sutton and Barto: Classic text on reinforcement learning
- **"Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications"** by Richard Szeliski: Comprehensive computer vision textbook

### Specialized Topics
- **"Humanoid Robotics: A Reference"** by Venture, Park, and Asano: Comprehensive reference on humanoid robotics
- **"Legged Robots That Balance"** by Raibert: Classic text on dynamic locomotion for legged robots
- **"Geometric Control of Mechanical Systems"** by Bullo and Lewis: Mathematical foundations for geometric control
- **"Mechanics and Control of Humanoid Robots"** by Nishiwaki and Kagami: Focuses on humanoid robot control
